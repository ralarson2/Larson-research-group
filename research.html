Anaerobic Digestion

We study anaerobic digestion systems that convert manure and other organic wastes into renewable biogas, while improving nutrient management and reducing environmental impacts.

Focus

Digester performance, co-digestion opportunities, operational stability, and outcomes that matter for farms and communities.

Applications

Renewable energy generation, methane mitigation from manure management, and integration with nutrient recovery and manure handling.
