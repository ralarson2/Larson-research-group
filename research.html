Research & Extension

Our projects include sustainable manure and organic waste systems, waste-to-energy pathways, nutrient recovery, and international initiatives.

Anaerobic Digestion

Research on converting organic waste to biogas to generate energy while reducing environmental impact.

Biochar

Investigating pyrolysis of manure and organic waste to produce biochar for soil amendment and carbon sequestration.

Manure Nutrient Recovery

Developing methods to recover nitrogen, phosphorus, and other nutrients from manure for sustainable fertilizer use.

Manure Management to Mitigate Climate Impacts

Exploring strategies to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from manure storage, treatment, and land application.

Uganda Air Quality

Monitoring and improving air quality from organic waste systems in international agricultural contexts.
