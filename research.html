Research & Extension

Our projects include sustainable manure and organic waste systems, waste-to-energy pathways, nutrient recovery, and international initiatives.

Anaerobic Digestion

Research on converting organic waste to biogas to generate renewable energy while reducing environmental impact.

Biochar

Investigating pyrolysis of manure and organic waste streams to produce biochar.

Manure Separation and Nutrient Recovery

Developing methods to separate manure components and recover nitrogen, phosphorus, and other products from manure.

Composting

Composting of manure and other organic feedstocks to produce a high-quality soil amendment.

Uganda Air Quality

Monitoring and improving air quality in Kampala, Uganda.

Manure Management to Mitigate Nitrogen Losses

Exploring strategies to reduce nitrogen loss from manure storage and land application.

Manure Land Application

Land application of manure.

Manure Storage

Storage of liquid, slurry, and solid manure. Agitation of liquid and slurry manure.

Manure Management to Mitigate Climate Impacts

Exploring strategies to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from manure storage and land application.
